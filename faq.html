Frequently Asked Questions

What is Danish's Portfolio about?

Danish's Portfolio showcases a collection of my work and projects, providing an overview of my skills and experience.

How can I contact you?

You can contact me via the contact form on the Contact page, or email me directly at mdanishateeq@gmail.com.

Do you offer freelance services?

Yes, I offer freelance services for various projects. Please reach out through the contact form for more details.

Can I see examples of your previous work?

Yes, examples of my previous work are available in the Portfolio section of the website. Feel free to explore the projects to get a sense of my style and expertise.

Do you offer consultations?

Yes, I offer consultations to discuss potential projects, ideas, or collaborations. Please get in touch via the Contact page to schedule a meeting.

What is your approach to project deadlines?

I prioritize timely delivery and aim to meet project deadlines as agreed upon with clients. If any issues arise, I communicate promptly to find a suitable solution.

What industries do you typically work with?

I work with a range of industries including technology, education, and e-commerce. If you have a specific industry in mind, feel free to reach out to discuss your needs.

How do you handle revisions?

I offer a certain number of revisions as part of the project scope. Additional revisions may incur extra charges. Details are usually specified in the project agreement.

What is your process for starting a new project?

My process typically involves an initial consultation to understand your requirements, followed by planning, design, development, and finally, delivery and review. I keep you updated throughout each stage.

Do you provide training for using the final product?

Yes, I provide training sessions to help you understand and use the final product effectively. Training details are included in the project scope.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept various payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and JazzCash. Payment details are discussed and agreed upon before the start of the project.

Can you help with website hosting?

While I primarily focus on design and development, I can provide recommendations for reliable hosting services and assist with the setup if needed.

What should I prepare before we start a project?

It’s helpful to have a clear idea of your goals, target audience, and any specific requirements or preferences. Providing content and examples of designs you like can also be beneficial.

How long does it take to complete a project?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. I provide an estimated timeline during the planning phase and keep you informed of progress along the way.

Do you offer support after the project is completed?

Yes, I offer post-launch support to address any issues or make adjustments as needed. Details of this support are outlined in the project contract.

How do I get started with a project?

To get started, please fill out the contact form on the Contact page or email me directly. We’ll schedule a consultation to discuss your project and next steps.
